Grub


(five bone award)

Since its conception in 2010, Grub Fresh Food has taken pride in offering people in the Aberdeen area a sustaining and wholesome lunch. Across our three Aberdeen sites and external catering service, we strive to provide both substance and quality at an affordable price, always sticking to our brand’s promise to give people fresh food with a difference. We fill our paninis, baguettes, wraps and salads with exciting flavour and combinations, which range from classic favourites to quirky pairings - always using fresh and locally sourced ingredients. Each morning, we make our homemade soup fresh on site, and serve it alongside expertly brewed coffees and a variety of cakes and healthy snacks.

Waterstone's


(four bone award)

Waterstones is changing. We are the last remaining chain of specialist bookshops on the high street, and with more ways to read than ever, as well as competition from things like computer games and the sheer mass of information in the digital space, we are challenged with making our shops welcoming and interesting places to browse and explore, offering outstanding service, and an unrivalled array of bookshop events. They are staffed with passionate, knowledgeable booksellers, selling the right range of books for the local market, and who are ready to recommend that book that you didn’t know you wanted. The local aspect is hugely important to us, and the “one size fits all” approach is firmly in the past. No two Waterstones bookshops are the same, with each shop tailoring its range to reflect the trends and interests of their local market, as well as the big national bestsellers that are in demand across the country. The local approach also extends to our Café W locations across the UK, where we bring you the best produce from local suppliers to cut down on food miles and support the local business community.
